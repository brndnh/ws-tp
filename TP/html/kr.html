days 14-21: seoul

influenced by my friend group's decision making to go to seoul after japan around 5 months before our trip, i decided learn the korean script hangul for fun— knowing beforehand that it was supposedly really easy to learn.

it took about a week of practice to fully be able to read everything, albeit at a very slow pace.

while i was in seoul, i could read everything that was around me but not understand any of it. its fascinating to me how intuitive the hangeul script is.
